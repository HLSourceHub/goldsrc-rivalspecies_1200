With two high pressurized nozzles and a dual piezo ignition system the Antares-pattern Heavy Flamer is a weapon to be reckoned with.
With an extensive dual-tank promethium fuel system, the weapon is most lethal in close quarters, where running from a lethal gout of fire is hardest.
Easily a faviorite for clearing bunkers and trenches, the promethium fuel even burns underwater.
